Paul Sunday Nnamchi



Member representing the good people of Enugu East & Isiuzo Federal Constituency.

Paul Nnamchi joined ESUT's Department of Metallurgical and Materials Engineering in 2019. He earned his PhD, Meng, and B.Eng. degrees from Sheffield University, ESUT, and Technical University of Delft in the Netherlands. Paul has held academic positions at Northumbria University, Sheffield University, and research institutes in Germany and the Netherlands. His research covers various metallic materials, including bulk metallic glasses and high entropy alloys. He's responsible for postgraduate programs and research group supervision, teaching material science, and engineering. Paul is a Chartered Engineer (UK), Chartered Scientist (UK), and an associate professor. His research spans biomaterial alloys, bio-inspired structures, and rapid manufacturing technologies. He's a Fellow of IOM3 (UK) and has presented his work at international conferences. Paul Nnamchi is also a member of several professional organizations, including COREN in Nigeria and the Institute of Physics UK.
